# Gumroad Pipeline

## Context

Use this skill when the user wants to create, launch, and monetize a digital product using Gumroad. This skill covers the full pipeline from product ideation through post-launch optimization, including email funnels, sales pages, and upsell sequences.

**Trigger phrases:** "launch a Gumroad product," "create a digital product funnel," "Gumroad sales page," "email nurture sequence," "lead magnet for Gumroad."

---

## Instructions

### Step 1: Product Ideation & Validation

1. **Identify the target audience.** Define:
   - Who they are (role, experience level, aspirations)
   - Their primary pain point or desired transformation
   - Where they hang out online (subreddits, Twitter communities, Discord servers)

2. **Brainstorm product formats** ranked by effort-to-value ratio:
   - Tier 1 (fast): Notion templates, cheat sheets, swipe files, checklists
   - Tier 2 (medium): eBooks, mini-courses (video), curated resource packs
   - Tier 3 (premium): comprehensive courses, community access, coaching add-ons

3. **Validate demand** before building:
   - Search Gumroad for competing products (note pricing and review counts)
   - Check Reddit/forums for recurring questions the product answers
   - Pre-sell: post a landing page and gauge email signups

### Step 2: Product Packaging & Pricing

4. **Create the product.** Follow packaging rules:
   - Deliver clear transformation, not just information
   - Include at least one actionable framework or template
   - Add bonuses (templates, checklists, quick-start guides)
   - Format professionally: PDF with consistent branding, clean typography

5. **Set pricing strategy:**
   - Minimum viable price: $9 for a cheat sheet, $19-29 for an eBook, $49-97 for a course
   - Use "pay what you want" only for lead magnets (minimum $0, suggested $5+)
   - Offer 3 tiers if possible: Basic, Standard, Premium (with coaching or extras)
   - Anchor high: show the premium price, make the standard feel like a deal

6. **Configure the Gumroad product page:**
   - Compelling product title (benefit-driven, not descriptive)
   - 3-5 bullet points highlighting key outcomes
   - Cover image or mockup (use Canva, not a plain text thumbnail)
   - Set up variants/tiers with clear value differentiation

### Step 3: Lead Magnet & Email Capture

7. **Create a free lead magnet** related to the paid product:
   - Must solve ONE specific problem completely
   - Format: 1-3 page checklist, mini template, or short PDF guide
   - Includes a natural upsell mention at the end (not a hard pitch)

8. **Set up the email capture:**
   - Gumroad's built-in email collection (requires email on free products)
   - Or embed a form (ConvertKit, Mailchimp, Beehiiv) on a simple landing page
   - Thank-you page redirects to download AND teases the paid product

### Step 4: Sales Page Copy

9. **Write the sales page** using this structure:
   ```
   HEADLINE: [Audience] + [Transformation] + [Timeframe]
   SUBHEADLINE: One sentence expanding on the promise

   PROBLEM: 2-3 paragraphs describing the pain point
   AGITATE: What happens if they don't solve it

   SOLUTION: Introduce the product as the answer
   FEATURES → BENEFITS: 5-7 bullet points, each Feature → So you can Benefit

   SOCIAL PROOF: Testimonials, results, or authority signals
   BONUS: What else they get (increases perceived value)

   FAQ: 3-5 objections addressed
   CTA: Clear, single action button
   GUARANTEE: 30-day refund or satisfaction promise
   ```

### Step 5: Email Funnel

10. **Build the nurture sequence:**
    - **Email 1 (Day 0 - Welcome):** Deliver the lead magnet, set expectations, share your story
    - **Email 2 (Day 2 - Value):** Teach one actionable lesson, build trust
    - **Email 3 (Day 4 - Agitate):** Expand on the problem, show the cost of inaction
    - **Email 4 (Day 6 - Soft pitch):** Introduce the product as the solution, share a testimonial
    - **Email 5 (Day 8 - Hard pitch):** Direct pitch with deadline or bonus incentive
    - **Email 6 (Day 12 - Final):** Last call, urgency, answer remaining objections

11. **Post-purchase sequence:**
    - Email 1: Delivery confirmation + getting-started guide
    - Email 2 (Day 3): Check-in, offer support
    - Email 3 (Day 7): Request a testimonial/review
    - Email 4 (Day 14): Upsell to premium tier or next product

### Step 6: Launch & Optimize

12. **Launch checklist:**
    - [ ] Product uploaded and preview tested
    - [ ] Sales page live and mobile-responsive
    - [ ] Email funnel active and tested
    - [ ] Payment processing verified (test purchase)
    - [ ] Social media posts scheduled
    - [ ] Share in 2-3 relevant communities (not spammy)

13. **Track these metrics weekly:**
    - Conversion rate: email subscribers → paid customers (target: 2-5%)
    - Email open rate (target: 40%+ for welcome, 25%+ for nurture)
    - Revenue per subscriber
    - Refund rate (target: < 5%)
    - Traffic sources (which channel drives the most buyers)

---

## Constraints

- NEVER make false income claims or use manipulative urgency ("only 3 left at this price").
- NEVER gate essential information behind paywalls; the paid product must provide convenience, structure, or depth.
- The lead magnet MUST deliver standalone value. It cannot be a teaser that requires purchase.
- Email frequency: maximum 3 emails per week during launch, 1 per week during nurture.
- ALWAYS honor refund requests within the stated policy period.
- Pricing MUST be transparent. No hidden fees, mandatory upsells, or "pay to unlock the real content."
- Social proof MUST be genuine. Never fabricate testimonials or inflate numbers.

## Examples

### Example 1: Developer Productivity Notion Pack

```
Product: "The DevOps Command Center" Notion Template Pack
Price: $19 (standard) / $39 (premium with video walkthrough)
Lead Magnet: Free "Deployment Checklist" PDF (1 page)
Funnel: 6-email sequence over 12 days
Audience: Junior-to-mid DevOps engineers
Sales page hook: "Ship with confidence. Stop googling deployment steps."
Metrics target: 4% conversion, $0.76 revenue/subscriber
```

### Example 2: Creator Email Course

```
Product: "Email That Converts" - 5-module video course for creators
Price: $47 (standard) / $97 (premium + 1:1 email review)
Lead Magnet: Free "10 Subject Line Formulas" swipe file
Funnel: 6-email sequence, launch-style with 48-hour open/close window
Audience: Newsletter creators with 500-5000 subscribers
Sales page hook: "Turn your 15% open rate into 45%. Here's the exact framework."
Upsell: "Subject Line Masterclass" ($29 add-on, offered post-purchase)
```
